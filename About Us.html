Frequently Asked Questions

What Financing Options Are Available?


We offer a variety of financing options to suit your needs. Financing can be arranged through our partnered finance companies or banks. Customers are eligible to finance up to 70% of the vehicle's value, with flexible repayment terms extending up to 5 years. Our finance specialists are on hand to provide you with tailored solutions and guide you through the process.

Can I Test Drive the Vehicle?


Yes, we encourage test drives! To ensure the vehicle is ready for your test drive experience, we kindly request a one-day notice. This allows us to prepare the vehicle and ensure it's out of the showroom and ready for your arrival.

Can I Trade in My Current Vehicle??


Absolutely! We welcome trade-ins and are committed to offering fair valuations. The trade-in value is contingent upon the condition of your current vehicle. We encourage you to bring your vehicle to our dealership for an assessment by our expert evaluators.

How Can I Contact You for Further Assistance?


We are here to assist you! For further inquiries or assistance, please feel free to contact us at 077 077 5807 or 0772 299003. Our dedicated customer service team is available to answer your questions and provide the support you need.
